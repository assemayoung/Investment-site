Property

The property is a medical and commercial building in a highly frequented location in the center of Bonn Bad Godesberg. The Wilhelminian style villa from 1890 is located in the Bad Godesberger Kurpark and was supplemented by a new pedestrian zone in 1955/1974. An integrated underground car park offers coveted parking space in the city center. Most recently, the property was partially modernized in 2012 and also continuously repaired. As part of the technical appraisal, the appraiser attested that it was in good, contemporary condition. This is also documented by the very high occupancy rate of the property.

Location

Bonn

As the former seat of government, Bonn is a prosperous center in the Rhineland metropolitan region, which, with 8.63 million inhabitants, is one of the largest metropolitan areas in Germany. After the government moved to Berlin, Bonn underwent a comprehensive transformation into an economic and scientific metropolis, with the headquarters of the two DAX groups Deutsche Telekom and Deutsche Post DHL as well as other dynamic companies such as Europe's largest confectionery manufacturer Haribo. In addition, Bonn is the headquarters of various ministries, other federal authorities and, with over 20 UN organizations, the second political center in the FRG. With 38,000 students, the Rheinische Friedrich-Wilhelms-Universität is one of the largest and most renowned universities in the country.

This results in a steady, positive growth trend in increasing numbers of employees and residents. The significantly above-average purchasing power index of 109.2 - in front of the megacity of Cologne - also documents the economic strength of its residents.
