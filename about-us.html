about us

This is Exporo

Exporo is Germany's leading platform for digital real estate investments. Under the motto "Investing in real estate easily and directly"It offers investors an overview of real estate projects in which they are involved simple, transparent and free of chargecan invest. Private investors can benefit from two complementary product categories:

On the one hand, they can participate in high-yield real estate projects that have been checked in advance at short notice and with a fixed interest rate (Exporo Finanzierung)- Here project developers erect e.g. new residential buildings or renovate commercial properties over a certain period of time.

And on the other hand, a real innovation, investors can directly access selected existing properties (Exporo Bestand)- similar to an owner - they receive quarterly distributions from the rental surpluses and are also involved in the increase in value.

Simon Brunke, Björn Maronde, Julian Oertzen and Tim Bütecke founded the online platform in 2014 and continuously developed it. Simon Brunke, Björn Maronde, Julian Oertzen and Carl-Friedrich von Stechow are board members of today's Exporo AG. The company now employs almost 200 people in Europe and is headquartered in Hamburg's Hafencity.

FINANCIAL EXPERTS ON THE SIDE OF EXPORO

THE EXPORO ADVISORY BOARD

Behind the vision of Exporo there is not only a strong team, but also an advisory board made up of top-class financial experts who are always on hand to provide advice and assistance.

Dr. Axel Wieandt is now a senior advisor, investor and non-executive board member in the financial sector with a focus on fintech. He has almost 30 years of experience in this sector, including as Head of Corporate Development at Deutsche Bank AG from 2001 to 2008, as CEO and CFO of Hypo Real Estate Holding AG / pbb Deutsche Pfandbriefbank AG in the financial crisis of 2008 to 2010 as well as CEO and CFO of Valovis Bank AG after the euro crisis from 2012 to 2015. Since 2005 he has also been honorary professor for financial intermediaries at the WHU Otto Beisheim School of Management in Vallendar. Since 2013 he has also been teaching bank management and digital assets / blockchain technology at the Goethe Business School at the University of Frankfurt. He has also been Adjunct Professor Finance at J.L. Kellogg Graduate School of Management in Evanston / Illinois. More details on his résumé

Dr. Hauke Brede is an experienced investment manager & amp; Senior advisor with an extensive network and great expertise in the field of "alternatives". For seven years he was Chief Risk Officer and member of the Global Executive and Investment Committee of Allianz Real Estate (ARE) in Munich, which at the time managed real estate investments amounting to around 55 billion euros. He started his career at the strategy consultancy of Deloitte Consulting, Braxton Associates, in the USA. After another position in the USA as Director at the VC Incubator Gen3 Partners, he moved to the Boston Consulting Group in Munich in 2001 as a senior project manager and core member of the German recruiting team. In 2006, Dr. Brede to Hypo (International) Bank AG, where he was Chief Operating Officer Credit Risk Management. At pbb Deutsche Pfandbriefbank AG, he was most recently Head of Business Management before moving to Allianz Real Estate in 2010.
