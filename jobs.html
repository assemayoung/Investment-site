about us

Exporo is Germany's market leader for digital real estate investments. Investors can easily and directly invest in professional real estate of their choice for as little as 1 EURO and thus build their own digital real estate portfolio while benefiting from returns of up to 6% annually.

In our centrally located office in Hamburg's HafenCity, we unite more than 180 clever and innovative minds with a wide variety of skills and ideas. Together we have set ourselves the goal of democratizing the European market for real estate investments through the use of expertise, data and technology. Due to our continuous growth, we also invite you to start your journey at Exporo and help make digital real estate investments a matter of course.

Your application to us

What do we expect from you?

If you like, you can send us a short and crisp letter of motivation. We are not a fan of long and old-fashioned cover letters.

Your CV should be well structured and set up in such detail that we can easily see whether you fit the advertised position.

You are welcome to contact us at any time if you have any questions.

Our recruiting process

What can you expect when you apply to us?

As soon as we have received your application, you will receive a confirmation of receipt and we will screen your documents.

The next step involves getting to know HR and the respective manager who has to fill the relevant role.

In the case of individual roles, a small task follows in which we challenge the know-how you have collected so far.

If everything has gone well by then, you will get to know your potential future team in a "Meet the Team".
